How to build a Z-stage for confocal microscopes from standard piezo buzzer?

As a Z-stack generator serves an Arduino Due, which is controlled by a PC via a self-written GUI. The output voltage of the Arduinos DAC is amplified by a self-made voltage amplifier and drives the piezo buzzer.
