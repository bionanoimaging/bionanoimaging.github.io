An autonomous camera was developed to support the alignment of optical setups by enabling integration into the Thorlabs cage system for precise on-axis placement. Wireless control via an app allows the camera to function as a reference for aligning optical components in the beam path. Development included the hardware design (housing and component integration) as well as the software for establishing connectivity and controlling the camera.
